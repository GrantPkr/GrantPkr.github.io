Welcome

Building intelligent systems that are practical, reliable, and thoughtfully designed.

Welcome to my portfolio. I am Grant Parker, a Computer Science student at the University of Colorado Boulder with a strong interest in artificial intelligence, machine learning, automation, and software engineering. I created this site to bring my work into one place that shows not only what I have built, but also how I think about solving problems. Beyond serving as a course project, this portfolio is meant to function as a clear and honest representation of my direction, my technical interests, and the kind of engineer I want to become.

A major reason I wanted to build this portfolio is that platforms like LinkedIn and GitHub are useful, but they do not fully let me tell a coherent story about my work. I wanted a space I could control, where visitors can quickly understand what I care about, what I have worked on, and what I have learned through building technical systems. My main audience includes recruiters, hiring managers, and engineers who may want to look more deeply at my projects. Because of that, I want this site to communicate reliability, clarity, and technical credibility. I want people to see that I care about building intelligent systems with engineering discipline, and that I think seriously about tradeoffs, practical impact, and responsible design.

Throughout this portfolio, you will find project highlights, academic and technical work, extracurricular interests, my resume, and links to platforms where I share my work more directly. I am especially interested in projects that connect intelligence with usability, meaning systems that are not only capable, but also robust enough to fit into real workflows. I also care about the ethical and social side of automation, and I want that perspective to be reflected in how I present my work. My goal is for this site to feel calm, structured, and professional, while still giving a personal sense of who I am and where I am headed.
